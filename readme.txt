-- Commands available are bellow ---------------------------------------
  1: make         - to hightlight words you search for in target.txt
  2: make get     - to output words you look for into result.txt
  2: make source  - to show all contents in source.txt
  3: make target  - to show all words in target.txt
  4: make ARGS="(word1)" search
     make ARGS="(word1)\|(word2)\|(word3)\|..." search
                  - to search word(s) you search for
